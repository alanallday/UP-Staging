What is Union Park?

Oxford Properties is starting to explore the redevelopment of Union Park at 325 Front Street. Surrounded by the CN Tower, Rogers Centre, and the Metro Convention Centre, this is an exciting opportunity to reimagine this iconic block in the heart of downtown Toronto.
